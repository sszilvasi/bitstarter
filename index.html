Technology

VumberJack is an mobile web service specialized in removing trees from virtual environments. Using the latest VumberJack technology, we offer the removal of trees, branches and leaves from both static and dynamic virtual environments at a reasonable price.

Pay by pixel

Targeting static virtual environments, such as digital photographs, VumberJack already offers silent and professional tree-removal with the flexible pay-by-pixel pricing model. Reduce the photo resolution to save money, and never pay more than the number of pixels modified!

Pay by vertices

For dynamic virtual surroundings where unwanted tress block movement or view VumberJackDynl is the ultimate solution. The pay-by-vertices plan makes the chop-down of high-resolution branches and low-vertex-count forests affordable for both small businesses and individuals.
